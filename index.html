Our Mission

At Hope Farm Foundation, we support local farmers ny reducing post-harvest losses in vegetables and cereals. We provide training in food processing and value addition to help individuals generate income and diversify their livehoods. Through our One Million Trees initiative, we also work with communities, schools, and local groups to restore the environment through tree planting.
