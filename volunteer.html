Meet our volunteers

Hear from some of our Hope Farm Foundation about their experiences volunteering.

Fatou, Kanifing

“Volunteering at Hope Farm Foundation has been one of the most rewarding experiences of my life. Working with farmers to improve crop yields through sustainable methods showed me how small actions can create lasting change in entire communities.”

Muhammed, Banjul

“I joined Hope Farm Foundation to help train young people in modern farming skills. What I didn’t expect was how much I would learn myself from teamwork, patience, and the joy of seeing others grow in confidence.”

Ebrima, Kiang

“Teaching rural families about organic farming and water conservation helped me understand the deep connection between people and the land. It’s amazing to see the positive environmental impact of our collective effort.”

Sainabou, Brikama

“Being part of the irrigation initiative was eye-opening. We built systems that now help farmers grow was a new learning experience. It’s a place where passion and purpose meet.”
